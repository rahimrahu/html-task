Maldives,Meeru Island

Meeru offers an extensive range of modern facilities, services, and activities for the guests to enjoy during their Maldivian holiday on the Island.Apart from the scenic Speed Boat Transfers to and from the Velana International Airport to Meeru, the island offers an array of Land and Water Sports and 2 Fresh Water Swimming Pools on the beach: the Dhoni Bar Pool with a Children’s Wading Pool and the Pavilion Bar Pool – the infinity pool for adults, age 18 years and older. There is also a TV Room, a Library, a fully equipped fitness centre and two Games Rooms: Kakuni Bar with Table Tennis, Billiards, Snooker, Foosball Table, Darts, Mahjong Table and Board Games and another Games Room at the Uthuru Bar with Billiards, Snooker, Foosball Table, Darts and Board Games.

Shopping on the Resort includes, Gift Shops with Internet Cafés featuring a complete selection of Swimwear, Sportswear, Batik, Meeru Logo wear, Books, Music CD’s, Jewelry, Artwork, Crafts, Sundries, Snacks, Magazines, Cigarettes, and more. For guests who are looking to surprise their loved one with jewelry, ‘Fine Jewels’ offers a complete selection of Gemstones and Jewelry at reasonable prices. The Euro-Divers SCUBA Center also features a dive shop.
